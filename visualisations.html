Zone Classification — Plant Explorer

Explore a fuel-storage and loading terminal with eight hazardous-area regions classified per IEC 60079-10-1 (gas) and IEC 60079-10-2 (dust). Click any numbered region to see how the zone is determined and what it demands of the equipment installed there.
