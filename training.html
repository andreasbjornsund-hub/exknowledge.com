Certified Training Providers

For formal qualifications, certifications, and instructor-led training. These organizations offer accredited courses recognized by industry.

CompEx (JTL)

International • Certification

Industry-standard competency certification for explosive atmospheres. CompEx courses (Ex01-Ex04, Ex11-Ex12) are required by many operators for installation and inspection work. Global network of accredited training centres.

ExVeritas

UK • Training & Consulting

Independent Ex certification consultancy offering training on ATEX, IECEx, and explosion protection. Courses range from awareness-level to detailed design and certification workshops.

Pepperl+Fuchs Academy

Germany • Manufacturer Training

Training on intrinsic safety, Ex i system design, and hazardous area sensor technology. Global training centres and online delivery. Free webinars available.

DEKRA Training

International • Certification Body

Courses on ATEX/IECEx requirements, hazardous area classification, and equipment selection. Practical lab sessions at their testing facilities.

TUV SUD Academy

Germany • Certification Body

ATEX awareness and specialist courses for manufacturers, installers, and plant operators. Covers directive requirements, protection concepts, and conformity assessment.

SGS Baseefa Training

UK • Certification Body

Courses on ATEX and IECEx certification, Ex equipment design, and compliance. Taught by engineers who carry out certification testing. Classroom and online options.

INERIS Training

France • Certification Body

Specialist courses on ATEX, process safety, dust explosion prevention, and risk assessment. Courses in French and English.

IECEx CoPC Scheme

International • Personnel Certification

IECEx Certificate of Personnel Competence. International recognition of Ex competency across design, installation, inspection, and maintenance units.

EEMUA Courses

UK • Industry Association

Courses on alarm management, mechanical integrity, and control of hazards. Relevant for plant engineers and operations staff working in process industries.

HSE DSEAR Guidance

UK • Regulator

Free guidance documents and Approved Codes of Practice for DSEAR compliance. Essential reading for UK-based engineers and compliance officers.
