About the podcast

ExKnowledge started as a free online reference covering 23 explosion protection topics in 11 languages. The podcast takes that same material and puts it in audio form. Each episode covers one topic in enough depth to be useful without reading the standard word by word.

All content is based on the IEC 60079 standard series, ATEX Directive 2014/34/EU, and IECEx operational documents. Episodes are 10 to 15 minutes each. No guests, no banter, just the technical content explained clearly.

For engineers working in oil and gas, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, mining, and food processing. Also for technical sales, HSE professionals, and anyone preparing for CompEx certification.
